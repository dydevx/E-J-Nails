About us

E & J Nails is a modern nail studio dedicated to precise shaping, polished finishes and a calm beauty experience. Our technicians work with carefully selected products and a clean process to create nails that feel elegant, durable and personal to your style.

From soft everyday colours to French tips, ombre, BIAB, SNS and detailed nail art, every appointment is handled with focused care so your hands and feet leave looking refined and fresh.
